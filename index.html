How to Play

Setup

  • Enter the names of your two contestants in the scoreboard
  • One person acts as the host and controls the game

The Timer Zone

  • Press the big red START button to begin a round
  • A hidden timer starts (between 1 second and 2 minutes)
  • The donut chart shows progress but you won't know exactly when time runs out!
  • When time's up, a loud siren will sound

Scoring

  • Use the +/- buttons to manually adjust scores anytime
  • After the siren, dismiss it to reveal the prize wheel
  • Set your desired point range (min: -20, max: 20)
  • Spin the wheel and award points to a contestant
  • Scores can go negative!

Winning

Play as many rounds as you like! The contestant with the most points at the end wins. But remember... in this game, anything goes!
